About the Academy

Happy Feet NYC/NJ,
led by Anitha Prakash

A community-centered dance academy helping students build confidence, learn choreography, and experience the joy of performing on stage.

From beginner-friendly drop-in classes to long-term showcase batches and corporate workshops — bringing together dancers of all ages and skill levels across the tri-state area.

Browse Classes Get in Touch

What makes this different?

  • Classes for both kids and adults across NYC & NJ
  • Long-term showcase batches ending in live stage performances
  • Workshops, pop-ups, intensives, and personal training
  • Practice videos and class updates — all in one organized portal
  • No WhatsApp chaos: announcements delivered directly to students
  • Corporate workshops for team events and celebrations
